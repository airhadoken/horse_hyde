THE FEW CHERZ BRIGHT:
Were we too busy raging to notice we'd been forsaken back in the 70's?
Waking from 30 year black-outs --  cruising Sunday morning streets
in the rain between soporific coffee chains in back water towns
where the things we loved are cheap on sale in low rent charity shops?
DVD player showing John Gage performance at the Armoury Show.
