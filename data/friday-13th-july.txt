DENIS POTTER:
Re-reading "Seeing the Blossom" the transcript of Denis Potter's
last ever tv interview. I remember watching the broadcast when
it went out, astonished at how frank he was, no fear or animosity,
no waves of anger or reluctance to be honest -- a breath of fresh air.
Particularly moving was his description of looking out his window
at what he knew would be the last blossom he would ever see --
